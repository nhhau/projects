The Story

When a person is diagnosed with MND, time is not on their side - most people still die within two and a half years of diagnosis.

This needs to change. We’re on a mission to supercharge the search for treatments that actually work, but in 2020, Covid-19 hit our fundraising hard. The Racing For MNDi Foundation funds pioneering genetic research into life changing treatments for MND, and we are working #againsttheclock to give back time. We’ve launched this appeal to top up the foundation coffers to coincide with MND Awareness Day on the 21st June. We’ve got 21 days to raise as much as we can to accelerate game-changing treatments for MND. Get involved in the #AgainstTheClockChallenge.

The Cause

Why We’re Here

MND is devastating. 150 years since it was discovered, it remains a terminal condition with no effective treatment or cure. 30 years ago, no inherited forms of MND had been identified. Now, there are over 40 known familial genetic mutations and more genes being implicated in sporadic forms. Despite this, genetic research is still chronically underfunded. We say, that sucks. We believe answers to unlocking new treatments will be found in researching inherited forms of MND and discovering the hidden genes driving the disease. Racing for MNDi is the only foundation that exclusively funds genetic based research to accelerate game-changing treatments for all forms of MND.
