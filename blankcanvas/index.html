SEEING IS BELIEVING

As the saying goes; a picture is worth a thousand words. We can describe the architectural intent of a building; the space, the light, the materials. How a design responds to an environment. What it would feel like to live there.

But words will never capture the imagination like evocative imagery.

At Blank Canvas, our passionate team of former architects, 3D artists and interior stylists create cinematic CGI still images, animation and vision films that inform and inspire.

ART & ARCHITECTURE

Faithfully representing plans in 3D is only part of the brief. At Blank Canvas, we create scenes that convey moods. From composition to lighting, texturing and styling, everything is considered and crafted in the pursuit of the perfect 3D image.

OUR CANVAS
