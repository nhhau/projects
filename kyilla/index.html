Welcome to Kyilla Primary School

An Independent Public School for over 300 Kindergarten to Year 6 children; ‘Building a strong foundation for life’ is our vision for all of our children. At Kyilla Primary School we are proud of our achievements. We have a proven track record for ensuring that children are given every opportunity to do their best academically, socially and physically. Children are taught and supported by experienced teachers and school support staff.

Teaching and Learning

READ MORE ABOUT LEARNING AT KYILLA

Kyilla has a strong focus on Literacy and Numeracy, providing students with the ability to develop the skills and knowledge needed to be competent to use these skills in the real world, now and into the future.

In the Australian Curriculum, English has three main areas: literacy, literature and language. Embedded in these strands are reading, writing, spelling, speaking and listening, and grammar and punctuation.
