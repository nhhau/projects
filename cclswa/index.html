Who we have helped

Martin’s story

Martin came to us with an eye watering $146,000 in unsecured credit card and personal loan debt. We believe banks should never allow people to get into this much debt when they can’t afford it. They should do correct assessments before debt takes over people’s lives.

We achieved total debt waivers of over $110,000 and a cash-in-hand refund of almost $7,000 by proving irresponsible lending. This reduces Martin’s debt considerably and was the best outcome he could achieve.
