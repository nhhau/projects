Target Audience Needs

The target audience for the Bob Hawke College website are parents and families in the local intake area as well as prospective teachers and staff. Also, parents outside the local intake area looking at the Gifted and Talented programs.

The new website will assist the target audience to:

  • find relevant and useful information on all aspects of the college
  • enrol (or register interest) with the college
  • book tours of the college
  • engage with the school online
  • feel confidence and pride in the college
