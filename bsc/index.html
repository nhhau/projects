Features

BSC Tools is currently in open Beta. After the open Beta period ends ends, you will need to hold a set amount of tokens to access advanced features. Different membership tiers will be available in the future.

Pair Explorer

Real time price graphs & transactions for your favorite pairs.

Pool Explorer

Search for new pools, add or remove liquidity in a pair, find the best gems and avoid the scams.

Multi Swap

View and interact with multiple pairs on a single screen.

Gem Hunters Network

A place for gem hunters to acollaboratet and find hot new projects.

Custom Indicator & Pinescript

Add custom indicator that suites your trading style.

Portfolio & Yield Optimizer

Track your portfolio in one place and suggest the best strategies forr maximum yields.
