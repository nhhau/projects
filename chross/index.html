What Chross Brings To The Table

We created Chross Homes & Developments with a vision to make our clients building experience an enjoyable and memorable one. It is our mission to eliminate the stress of the building process and celebrate the exciting moments with our clients.

Being a small family owned and operated company we hold family values in high regard and our vision for Chross is to share those values with our clients, treat them the way we would like to be treated, and take pride in providing a home of the highest quality. Our clients can also expect honest and personalised service every step of the way.

At Chross, the Directors will be by your side throughout the entire process, offering you guidance and advice from initial meeting right through to key handover.

What We Offer

We offer all aspects of residential construction as well as flexibility with design and specification.

  • Custom Homes
  • Developments
  • Renovations/Extensions

We have no standard house designs, all of our plans are completely custom to each client or bring your own!

Our specification offers quality and variety however we understand that your house needs to be perfect for you, so create your wish list and we’ll incorporate this into your own personalised specification.

Meet The Directors

Chross Homes & Developments was formed in May 2020 by the Directors Chris Hardwick and Oscar Pit, who have a combined 25+ years experience in the building industry.

Oscar and Chris were first introduced to each other in 2010 when they both started working at a Perth project builder. Their friendship forged by their mutual respect for each other’s work ethic, professionalism and love of basketball. As they both moved on to different opportunities, they remained in contact mainly through their Perth Wildcats memberships and discussions began early on about a possible partnership.

Fast forward to 2020 and Chross Homes & Developments was born!
